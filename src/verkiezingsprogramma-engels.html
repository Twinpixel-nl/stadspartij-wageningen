English Summary – Election Programme 2026–2030

If Wageningen is dear to you. (als Wageningen je lief is)

Introduction

Wageningen is a city with a unique character: historic, green, a high diversity of people and forward-looking. It is a city where people know each other, where there is room for knowledge and innovation, but also for tranquility, connection, and engagement.

Stadspartij Wageningen is committed to preserving and strengthening these qualities. We believe in a liveable, accessible, and welcoming city in which residents, entrepreneurs, students, and visitors feel at home.

Our choices are realistic, transparent, and financially responsible. We do what we promise in cooperation with residents, organisations, and institutions that together make up the city.

Our mission

Stadspartij Wageningen wants to be a municipality that works with and for its residents. We place Wageningen’s character at the centre: diverse, engaged, and future-oriented.

Our vision

We are building a city where history and innovation reinforce each other, where people can live affordably, where nature and knowledge come together, and where everyone can participate. Wageningen must remain a city that lives, grows, and connects – without losing its identity.

Our core values

  • Liveability – A city where living, working, and relaxing are in balance.
  • Accessibility – Mobility that is logical, safe, and sustainable.
  • Hospitality – A city that is open to meeting, culture, and diversity.
  • Inclusive – Everyone participates!
  • Engagement – A government that listens, is visible, and works together.
  • Responsibility – Policies that are feasible, fair, and financially sound.

Closing statement

Stadspartij Wageningen chooses a course that stays close to its residents. We are building a city that respects its history, embraces its future, and where everyone feels at home.

Together, we keep the ‘Wageningen character’ alive. If Wageningen is dear to you. (als Wageningen je lief is)
